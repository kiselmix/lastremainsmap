Loot and regions

Master challenges added sporadically, earning XP and coins (Heads) along the way. Exchange heads for exclusive items in the shop! Quests 13th April Alpha playtest:

1. Find a dog (these do not move they just lay around places)

Toy dogs can be found in Buildings 30-35

2. Find two or more zombies trough a peep hole


3. Find a PC under a desk that is placed the other way around


4. Find what looks like a bottle of "Jack Daniels"

The bottles are on the kitchen units.

5. Find a dead zombie nurse inside the Hospital

Be careful, there are a lot of zombies in the hospital.

6. Go to the top floor of the construction site


7. Go inside the armory in the Police Station

Armory is often closed. You will have to make many attempts to get there. Building 617
